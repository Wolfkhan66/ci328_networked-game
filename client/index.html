How To Play

Frog: your aim is to collect as many coins as possible, whilst avoiding being captured by the ghosts.
Ghosts: your mission is to catch the frog, you will then become a frog and have the chance to collect as many coins as you can before being caught.
Either character can collect the mystery powerups - they might double your speed and points or they could be halved, the risk is yours!

Use the directional buttons to move, collect as many points as you can before time runs out.
